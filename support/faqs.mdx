# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using AutoLog for fleet management and asset tracking.

## Authentication & Access

<Accordion title="How do I log into AutoLog?">
  AutoLog supports both public and private sessions. For private sessions:
  1. Navigate to the login page
  2. Enter your email and password
  3. If 2FA is enabled, you'll be prompted for a verification code
  4. You'll be redirected to the home dashboard
  
  AutoLog uses secure httpOnly cookies for session management, ensuring your credentials are protected.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)?">
  2FA adds an extra layer of security to your account. When enabled:
  - You'll need to provide a verification code after entering your password
  - Codes are generated by an authenticator app on your phone
  - This prevents unauthorized access even if your password is compromised
  
  Enable 2FA in Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
  If you've forgotten your password:
  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Check your email for a reset link
  4. Follow the link to create a new password
  
  Administrators can also reset passwords for users from the Users management page.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are login alerts?">
  Login alerts notify you when your account is accessed from a new device or location. This security feature helps you:
  - Monitor account activity
  - Detect unauthorized access attempts
  - Stay informed about login patterns
  
  Configure login alerts in Settings > Security > Login Alerts.
</Accordion>

## Vehicle Management

<Accordion title="How do I add a new vehicle to the system?">
  To add a vehicle:
  1. Navigate to the Vehicles section
  2. Click the "Add Vehicle" button
  3. Fill in vehicle details (make, model, VIN, license plate, etc.)
  4. Assign the vehicle to a company or location if needed
  5. Save the vehicle
  
  <Note>
    Each vehicle is automatically assigned a QR code that can be used for quick access and usage tracking.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I edit vehicle information?">
  Yes! To edit vehicle details:
  1. Go to the Vehicles list
  2. Find the vehicle you want to edit
  3. Click the edit icon or open the vehicle drawer
  4. Update the necessary information
  5. Press Enter or click Save to confirm changes
  
  <Warning>
    A bug was fixed in v2.3.2 that prevented saving when pressing Enter in the edit modal. Make sure you're on the latest version.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the vehicle QR code feature?">
  Each vehicle in AutoLog has a unique QR code that allows:
  - Quick vehicle identification
  - Scanning to log usage records
  - Mobile access to vehicle information
  - Streamlined check-in/check-out processes
  
  Access the QR code from the vehicle detail page and print or display it in the vehicle.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I track vehicle usage?">
  Vehicle usage can be tracked through:
  - Manual usage logs entered by drivers
  - QR code scanning for check-in/check-out
  - Location tracking if GPS devices are installed
  - Historical usage reports in the Reports section
</Accordion>

## Asset Tracking & Inventory

<Accordion title="How does asset inventory management work?">
  AutoLog's inventory system (introduced in v2.3.1) provides:
  - Complete asset list with filtering and sorting
  - Pagination for large inventories
  - Multi-language support (Spanish/English)
  - Asset status tracking (Active, In Transit, Maintenance, Backup)
  - Assignment to clients, sites, and locations
  
  Access the inventory from the Assets section in the main navigation.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I import assets in bulk?">
  Yes! AutoLog supports bulk asset import:
  1. Prepare your asset data in the required format
  2. Navigate to Assets > Import
  3. Upload your file
  4. Review and confirm the import
  
  <Note>
    As of v1.1.4, asset movements are processed sequentially to prevent failures with large batches.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What asset statuses are available?">
  AutoLog supports multiple asset statuses:
  - **Active**: Asset is in use
  - **In Transit**: Asset is being moved between locations
  - **Maintenance**: Asset is under repair or maintenance
  - **Backup**: Asset is available as a backup (added in v1.1.3)
  - **Retired**: Asset is no longer in service
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I assign assets to clients?">
  To assign assets to clients:
  1. Open the asset detail page or drawer
  2. Select the client from the dropdown
  3. Choose the specific site/location if applicable
  4. Add any relevant description
  5. Save the assignment
  
  The client assets page provides a visual overview of all assets assigned to each client.
</Accordion>

## Reports & Data

<Accordion title="What types of reports can I generate?">
  AutoLog offers various reporting capabilities:
  - Vehicle usage reports
  - Asset inventory reports
  - Movement history (Kardex)
  - Client assignment reports
  - Custom reports with filtering options
  
  Access reports from the Reports section in the main menu.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I filter and sort data?">
  Yes! Most data views in AutoLog support:
  - Search by keyword
  - Column sorting (ascending/descending)
  - Status filters
  - Date range filters
  - Company/location filters
  - Export to various formats
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I export data?">
  To export data:
  1. Navigate to the relevant section (Vehicles, Assets, etc.)
  2. Apply any desired filters
  3. Click the Export button
  4. Choose your preferred format
  5. The file will be downloaded to your device
</Accordion>

## Settings & Customization

<Accordion title="Can I customize the appearance of AutoLog?">
  Yes! AutoLog's theme engine (v2.1.0+) allows:
  - Custom color schemes
  - Font selection and application
  - Light/dark mode with automatic sync
  - Live preview of appearance changes
  - CSS variable customization for advanced users
  
  Customize appearance in Settings > Appearance.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is AutoLog available in multiple languages?">
  Yes! AutoLog supports internationalization (i18n):
  - **Spanish (ES)**: Full support
  - **English (EN)**: Full support
  
  Language settings are available throughout the application including Dashboard, Settings, Users, and Support sections.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I manage user permissions?">
  User permissions are managed through:
  1. Navigate to Settings > Users
  2. Select the user you want to modify
  3. Open the User Permissions Drawer
  4. Configure access levels for different modules
  5. Save changes
  
  <Note>
    Permission changes are cached. Use timestamp parameters in GET requests to prevent browser caching issues.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What can I configure in the Settings module?">
  The Settings module (completely redesigned in v2.3.0) includes:
  - **Account**: Personal profile and preferences
  - **Security**: Password, 2FA, login alerts
  - **Appearance**: Theme customization
  - **Companies**: Company management
  - **Locations**: Site and location setup
  - **Users**: User management and permissions
  - **System**: General system configuration
</Accordion>

## Companies & Locations

<Accordion title="How do I manage multiple companies?">
  AutoLog supports multi-company management:
  1. Navigate to Settings > Companies
  2. Add new companies with relevant details
  3. Assign vehicles and assets to companies
  4. Set up company-specific locations
  5. Configure permissions per company
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the difference between sites and locations?">
  - **Sites**: Physical locations where assets are deployed (e.g., customer facilities)
  - **Locations**: Internal organizational locations (e.g., warehouses, offices)
  
  Both can be used to track asset positions and movements within your organization.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I track asset movements between locations?">
  Yes! AutoLog maintains a complete movement history:
  - Origin and destination tracking
  - Movement timestamps
  - User who initiated the movement
  - Reason for movement
  - Kardex-style inventory tracking
  
  View movement history in the Assets > History section.
</Accordion>

## Search & Navigation

<Accordion title="How do I use the global search feature?">
  Access global search with **Ctrl+K** (Cmd+K on Mac):
  - Search across vehicles, assets, companies, locations
  - Search results are highlighted
  - Quick navigation to specific records
  - Recent searches are saved
  
  The search interface was updated in v1.1.2 with improved highlighting.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are keyboard shortcuts available?">
  AutoLog supports several keyboard shortcuts:
  - **Ctrl+K** / **Cmd+K**: Open global search
  - **Esc**: Close modals and drawers
  - **Enter**: Save forms in drawers (fixed in v1.2.1)
  
  More shortcuts may be available in specific modules.
</Accordion>
